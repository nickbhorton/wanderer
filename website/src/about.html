What is Wanderer?

Wanderer is a small website project aimed at showcasing ultra marathon portfolios.

Why only ultra marathons?

There are many featureful web apps and phone apps that are meant for all types of activities. This is not meant to be a social media app. Trust me, Strava is a much better place to share sub-ultra runs and all other types of activities.

Why use Wanderer?

Being an ultra marathoner myself, I feel that apps like Strava and Garmin Connect do not put effort into showcasing ultra marathon effort activities. This makes sense as most users will never run further than a marathon. Therefor spending time and money developing cool features for ultra marathoners will not recoup there investment. So the pitch is Wanderer will have cool analysis and features for ultra activites. The trade off is it will lack features that could more easily be aqired via another app.
